The Tranquil Greenway

Woodridge Estate

Nestled beneath a canopy of mature trees and framed by sculpted hedges, this lush green corridor offers a peaceful escape within Woodridge Estate. The Garden Walk

Woodridge Estate

This quiet, leafy pathway offers a secluded retreat within Woodridge Estate. Framed by brick boundary walls and a variety of indigenous trees and shrubs, the Garden Walk is ideal for a peaceful afternoon stroll or a moment of reflection. Thoughtfully maintained and softly shaded, it reflects the estate’s commitment to green living and resident well-being.

The Courtyard Garden

Woodridge Estate

A sunny, open lawn framed by trimmed hedges, palms, and native trees, the Courtyard Garden is a welcoming communal space for quiet reflection, reading, or catching up with neighbours. With natural seating under shade and soft landscaping, it captures the estate’s blend of serenity and social connection.

SpringRidge Recreation Lawn

Shared Facility for Woodridge & Springwood

Situated within the SpringRidge Recreation Centre, this open lawn space is part of the shared community amenities that include the tennis courts, children’s play area, and clubhouse. Ideal for informal gatherings, games, or simply relaxing under the trees, this green zone complements the active lifestyle offerings available to both Woodridge and Springwood residents.

SpringRidge Pool & Clubhouse

Shared Community Hub

At the heart of the SpringRidge Recreation Centre lies the sparkling pool and clubhouse — a vibrant hub for residents of Woodridge and Springwood. Whether enjoying a swim, hosting a gathering, or relaxing under the shaded pergola, this space offers a welcoming environment for leisure, connection, and community events. Maintained for all to enjoy, it reflects the shared commitment to quality living and recreational balance.
